Confirmed program facts

A connected group-learning environment

The program connects cultural identity, community, Islamic character and ethical behaviour. Exact group size, timetable, duration and curriculum units are not yet published.

Group program at a glance

  • Group classes
  • Children and teenagers aged 5–16
  • 40-minute lessons
  • Free 40-minute trial
  • Online participation
  • Not offered to adult women at launch

Connected themes

Heritage, community, character and everyday ethics

The learning environment should encourage positive participation and help children connect ideas with everyday life without guilt-based or fear-based teaching.

Identity

Explore a confident connection with Afghan cultural heritage.

Community

Understand belonging, family connection and respect for others.

Character

Learn practical Islamic manners and positive habits.

Ethics

Consider thoughtful, age-appropriate choices in everyday life.
