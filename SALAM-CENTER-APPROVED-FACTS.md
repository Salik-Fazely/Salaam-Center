# Salaam Center Approved Facts

This document is the concise source of truth for the Salaam Center English pre-launch website. Anything listed as unresolved must not be guessed or published as a final promise.

## 1. Approved brand facts

- Brand name: **Salaam Center**
- Intended domain: `https://salaam.center`
- Market category: online Afghan heritage education platform
- Launch language: English
- Future language phase: Dari/Persian with proper RTL support, not part of this launch
- Main message: **Help your child stay connected to their language, faith, and Afghan heritage.**
- Core belief: children should not have to lose their heritage simply because they grow up outside Afghanistan.

The intended domain is not connected during the pre-launch foundation. `salaamcenter.org` is not an approved domain.

## 2. Approved positioning

Salaam Center connects:

- Dari/Persian language
- Quran learning
- Afghan identity and culture
- Islamic faith, manners and character
- Afghan educators
- online accessibility
- parent involvement
- age-appropriate learning

Quran is an important program, but Salaam Center is not positioned only as a Quran school. The parent is the hero and Salaam Center is the guide. Messaging should provide reassurance, clarity and a practical path without guilt, fear or hostility toward the societies where families live.

## 3. Approved audience

Primary audience: Afghan parents and families living in Europe, the United States and Canada who want children growing up abroad to maintain a meaningful connection with family language, Afghan identity, cultural heritage, Islamic values, Quran learning, grandparents and relatives.

Secondary audience: women of any age who are eligible for private Quran or Dari/Persian lessons.

## 4. Approved programs

### Quran

- Online

- Free trial available

### Dari/Persian

- Online
- Private one-to-one
- Free trial available

### Afghan Culture & Islamic Ethics

- Online
- Group classes
- Free trial available
- Must not be described as life coaching, therapy or religious counselling

These are the only three approved launch programs.

## 5. Approved learner eligibility

- Quran: children and teenagers aged 5–16; women of any age
- Dari/Persian: children and teenagers aged 5–16; women of any age
- Afghan Culture & Islamic Ethics: children and teenagers aged 5–16; not an adult women’s program at launch

## 6. Approved lesson duration

Every free trial is 40 minutes. Every paid class is 40 minutes.
