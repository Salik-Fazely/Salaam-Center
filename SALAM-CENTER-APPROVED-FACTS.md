# Salaam Center Approved Facts

This document is the concise source of truth for the Salaam Center English pre-launch website. Anything listed as unresolved must not be guessed or published as a final promise.

## 1. Approved brand facts

- Brand name: **Salaam Center**
- Intended domain: `https://salaam.center`
- Market category: online Afghan heritage education platform
- Launch language: English
- Future language phase: Dari/Persian with proper RTL support, not part of this launch
- Main message: **Help your child stay connected to their language, faith, and Afghan heritage.**
- Core belief: children should not have to lose their heritage simply because they grow up outside Afghanistan.

The intended domain is not connected during the pre-launch foundation. `salaamcenter.org` is not an approved domain.

## 2. Approved positioning

Salaam Center connects:

- Dari/Persian language
- Quran learning
- Afghan identity and culture
- Islamic faith, manners and character
- Afghan educators
- online accessibility
- parent involvement
- age-appropriate learning

Quran is an important program, but Salaam Center is not positioned only as a Quran school. The parent is the hero and Salaam Center is the guide. Messaging should provide reassurance, clarity and a practical path without guilt, fear or hostility toward the societies where families live.

## 3. Approved audience

Primary audience: Afghan parents and families living in Europe, the United States and Canada who want children growing up abroad to maintain a meaningful connection with family language, Afghan identity, cultural heritage, Islamic values, Quran learning, grandparents and relatives.

Secondary audience: women of any age who are eligible for private Quran or Dari/Persian lessons.

## Approved public commercial facts

- Every paid class and every free trial lasts 40 minutes.
- One free 40-minute trial per learner is available without payment or an obligation to buy, subject to teacher and schedule availability. It does not guarantee a teacher or time.
- For a minor, a parent or guardian arranges the trial and remains the primary contact. An adult woman learner may be the primary contact.
- After the trial and confirmation of teacher and schedule availability, the learner or family reviews the selected plan, complete terms and payment information.
- Paid plans are paid in full before the first paid class. There is no saved-card subscription, public checkout or instalment plan. **No automatic renewal.**
- 4-week-plan teaching runs for four scheduled weeks and has final validity of **6 weeks from the first paid class**. The additional time is only for eligible rescheduled classes.
- 12-week-plan teaching runs for twelve scheduled weeks and has final validity of **16 weeks from the first paid class**. The additional time is only for eligible rescheduled or make-up classes.
- With at least 24 hours' notice, an affected class may be rescheduled once, subject to teacher availability and plan expiry, without being treated as used.
- With less than 24 hours' notice, a 4-week-plan class is treated as used. A 12-week plan includes no more than four late-cancellation make-up credits across the entire plan; once used, later late cancellations are treated as used.
- A no-show without communication is treated as used and does not automatically consume a 12-week make-up credit.
- A teacher or Salaam Center cancellation is rescheduled without using a learner class or make-up credit. Teacher or Salaam Center technical failure is also rescheduled.
- Late arrival does not extend the scheduled end time or create a make-up entitlement. Learner-side technical problems are reviewed according to circumstances.
- Both plan lengths include private one-to-one lessons, parent or adult-learner communication, a private family learning communication space, suitable interactive learning activities and teacher-cancellation protection.
- Additional 12-week benefits are 12 short Culture & Character mini-lessons, four late-cancellation make-up credits, extended quizzes and revision activities, eligible completion recognition, a progress summary every four weeks and supplementary educational videos.
- The Culture & Character mini-lessons are short supplementary digital or guided resources. They are not additional private 40-minute live classes and do not replace the Afghan Culture & Islamic Ethics group program.
- Digital certificate eligibility is limited to the 12-week plan. At least 80% of scheduled paid classes must be completed and any outstanding payment obligation must be resolved. It acknowledges participation and completion and is not academic accreditation, a qualification or a government-recognised certificate.
- Progress summary every four weeks: for a minor, it is shared with the parent or guardian; an adult learner receives it directly. It may cover attendance, areas practised, teacher observations and suggested next steps without promising formal assessment or outcomes.
- Refund and consumer-withdrawal terms remain pending legal and accounting review and must be shown before payment.

## Approved internal teacher compensation — not approved for public use

This is internal business information and is not approved for public use. Teacher compensation is €1.50 for each eligible 40-minute lesson.

Eligible compensation events are:

- a completed paid private lesson;
- a completed free trial lesson;
- a learner late cancellation or no-show when the class is treated as used, provided the teacher was present and available to teach.

Compensation is not added when the teacher cancels, the same class is later delivered as a replacement, a class was never scheduled, the teacher was unavailable, or a teacher-caused technical problem requires the class to be repeated. A teacher-cancelled class is compensated only when its replacement is delivered; the original cancellation and replacement are not both paid.

Each completed free 40-minute trial costs Salaam Center €1.50 in direct teacher compensation. This is a separate acquisition cost outside the paid-class calculations, and the trial remains free to the learner.
