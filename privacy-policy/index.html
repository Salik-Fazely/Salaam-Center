Pre-launch privacy notice

Privacy information before enrolment opens

This notice describes the website’s current inactive state and the limited trial-request data planned for launch. It is not the final production privacy policy.

Current website status

  • No live booking submissions are accepted.
  • No payment collection is active.
  • No analytics are used.
  • No advertising trackers are used.
  • No active Formspree endpoint is connected.

hello@salaam.center is the intended public contact address, but operational mailbox verification remains pending.

Planned trial-request information

The disabled preview form shows the data planned for a future request submitted by an adult contact. It is not active. The planned categories are:

  • Adult contact role, name and email
  • Country and time zone or nearest city
  • Learner first name and learner age group
  • Selected program and preferred frequency
  • Preferred availability
  • Optional current level and goals information
  • Privacy acknowledgement record
  • Technical submission metadata supplied by the form provider

Users should not submit medical information, diagnoses, government identifiers, financial information, passwords or other highly sensitive personal information.

Video samples

Pages with video samples use YouTube-hosted thumbnails. A privacy-enhanced YouTube player is created only after you choose to play a video.

Required before production launch

The final controller identity and controller postal address remain pending. The final production policy must also document the applicable legal bases, retention periods, Formspree/provider disclosure, international-transfer information where applicable, the data-rights process, complaint and supervisory-authority information, and the final operational contact process.

No final legal basis, retention period, transfer guarantee, legal entity or postal address is asserted by this pre-launch notice.
