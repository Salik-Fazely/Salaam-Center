Current non-contractual direction

This summary explains how the proposed private lesson plans are intended to operate while booking and payment remain inactive.

Plan basis

  • Plans are per learner and per program.
  • Classes are 40 minutes.
  • Plans are paid in full before paid lessons begin.
  • No automatic renewal.
  • Scheduling is confirmed before payment.

Scheduling summary

  • At least 24 hours’ notice allows an affected class to be rescheduled once, subject to availability and plan expiry.
  • Late cancellations follow different 4-week and 12-week rules.
  • Teacher cancellations are rescheduled without using a learner class or make-up credit.
  • Final validity allows a limited period for eligible rescheduled or make-up classes; unused classes then expire.
