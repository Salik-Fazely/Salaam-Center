Parent-managed communication

Families remain involved

For learners under 18, a parent or guardian is the primary contact. Scheduling and important learning decisions are managed through the parent or guardian.

Teacher privacy and learner safety

Teacher profiles do not publish phone numbers, personal email addresses, messaging links or social accounts. Website wording does not encourage children to contact teachers privately.
