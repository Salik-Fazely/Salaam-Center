Pre-launch privacy notice

Pre-launch privacy information

This notice explains the current WhatsApp handoff and website media behavior. It is not the final production privacy policy because the final controller identity, address and other legal details have not been approved.

What the Salaam Center website does

  • The trial form processes the entered values locally in your browser to prepare a plain-text WhatsApp message.
  • The Salaam Center website does not submit or store the trial-form information on a Salaam Center server.
  • No payment collection is active.
  • No analytics are used.
  • No advertising pixels are active.

When information moves to WhatsApp

The prepared message remains local until you deliberately choose to open WhatsApp. At that point, the text is included in the WhatsApp link so WhatsApp can display it for review. It is not delivered to Salaam Center as a conversation message unless you then press Send inside WhatsApp.

WhatsApp is a third-party service operated separately from Salaam Center. WhatsApp may process account, device, communication and technical information under its own policies. Review the prepared message before sending and review WhatsApp’s own privacy information for its service.

Keep the message limited and safe

Do not include medical information, identification numbers, financial information, passwords or other highly sensitive information. For a learner under 18, the parent or guardian must be the contact person and should review the message before sending.

Video samples

Pages with video samples use YouTube-hosted thumbnails. A privacy-enhanced YouTube player is created only after you choose to play a video. Video playback remains user activated.

Required before production launch

The final controller identity and controller postal address remain pending. The final policy must be legally reviewed and approved before this pre-launch notice can become the final production privacy policy.

This notice does not assert a final legal basis, data location, international-transfer guarantee, encryption scope, retention period, legal entity or postal address.
