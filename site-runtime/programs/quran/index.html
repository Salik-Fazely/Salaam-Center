Quran program

Private online Quran learning in a supportive environment

The Quran program offers private one-to-one lessons for Afghan children aged 5–16 and women of any age.

Confirmed program facts

A private format shaped around the learner

The learning direction will consider the learner’s current level and goals. This does not imply a formal diagnostic assessment or a fixed curriculum.

Quran program at a glance

  • Private one-to-one
  • Children and teenagers aged 5–16
  • Women of any age
  • 40-minute lessons
  • Free 40-minute trial
  • Online participation

Supportive learning

A positive relationship with Quran learning

Lessons should meet the learner at an appropriate stage, support steady participation and provide a respectful environment. No fixed progress timeline or memorisation outcome is promised, and the program does not claim academic accreditation or a qualification.

For children and teenagers

Parents and guardians remain the primary contact for scheduling, communication and important learning decisions.

For women

Women of any age are eligible for the same private one-to-one online format.

Private plan options

Choose a consistent Quran-learning rhythm

Private plans are available at 1, 2 or 4 classes per week, with flexible 4-week and 12-week plans. Every paid class is 40 minutes.

Pricing is per learner and for one selected program. The first 40-minute trial is free, with no obligation to purchase a plan.

Before a paid plan
