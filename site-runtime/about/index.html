About Salaam Center

Helping Afghan children abroad remain connected to their roots

Salaam Center is an online Afghan heritage education platform created for families raising children outside Afghanistan.

Our belief

A confident future can include a strong connection to heritage

We believe children can grow confidently within the societies where they live without losing connection with their family language, faith, cultural identity and values.

Our launch programs bring together Quran learning, Dari/Persian, and Afghan Culture & Islamic Ethics.

Children should not have to lose their heritage simply because they grow up outside Afghanistan.

Afghan educators

Sharing knowledge across borders

Salaam Center also supports Afghan women educators by creating opportunities for them to share their knowledge, skills and commitment with families around the world.

A connected education platform

  • Dari/Persian language
  • Quran learning
  • Afghan culture
  • Islamic character and ethics
  • Parent involvement

Learning environment

Respectful, supportive and parent-involved

Salaam Center is designed to provide a respectful, supportive learning environment. Parents and guardians remain involved in communication, scheduling and important decisions for learners under 18.
