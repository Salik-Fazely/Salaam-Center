How it works

A clear path from first conversation to the right class

Explore the available programs, understand the class format in a free 40-minute trial and receive a recommended next direction before enrolment.

Five clear steps

From exploration to learning

  1. Explore the programs

    Review available formats and learner eligibility.

  2. Attend a free 40-minute trial

    The trial gives the learner and family an opportunity to understand the teaching environment and discuss the most suitable direction.

  3. Receive an initial recommendation

    Salaam Center recommends an appropriate program and class format based on the information available.

  4. Confirm the teacher, schedule and plan

    Private learners confirm a suitable teacher and schedule before selecting a plan. Group placement depends on a suitable published cohort.

  5. Review payment information and begin learning

    Complete terms and payment information are reviewed before a private learner pays and begins scheduled lessons.

Availability comes before plan selection

The plan is selected after teacher and schedule availability are confirmed. Payment is made before paid lessons begin. No automatic online enrolment is currently active. Group enrolment follows a published cohort schedule when one becomes available.

Clear expectations

A recommendation, not a guarantee

Availability, schedules and teacher or group placement depend on the information and options available when enrolment opens.

What this process does not promise

  • No instant teacher matching
  • No guaranteed schedule
  • No immediate availability
  • No automatic enrolment

For learners under 18

Parents and guardians remain involved

A parent or guardian is the primary contact, manages scheduling and remains involved in important learning decisions. Salaam Center does not encourage children to contact teachers privately.
